⏰ Working Hours & Flexibility

Standard Hours

As agreed in your employment contract. Hours may be full-time, part-time, or flexible based on your role.

Breaks

You are entitled to a 20-minute break for every 6 hours worked. Breaks are paid and must be taken during your shift.

🏖️ Annual Leave

Employees: 20 days paid annual leave per year (minimum)

Workers: 5.6 weeks paid annual leave per year

Book leave with Sat as soon as possible. Leave is subject to business needs.

🏥 Sickness & Absence

Tell Sat immediately if you're unwell — don't just not show up.

Employees: Entitled to Statutory Sick Pay (SSP) if off sick for 3+ days

Workers: Are NOT entitled to SSP (employees only)

For absence longer than 7 days, provide a doctor's letter (Fit Note).

🏋️ Manual Handling & Warehouse Safety

All warehouse staff must complete manual handling training before handling goods over 20kg.

Required Training Course

High Speed Training — Manual Handling Course

  • Duration: 3 hours (online, self-paced)
  • Cost: £30 + VAT
  • Certificate: Awarded upon completion
  • Link: High Speed Training Manual Handling

What you'll learn:

  • How to lift safely without injuring your back
  • Risks of improper manual handling
  • Best practices for warehouse work
  • When to ask for help

Action: Once you've completed the course, download your certificate and email to sat@byerim.com. Keep it as proof of training.

🔐 Confidentiality & Data Protection

Keep the following confidential:

  • Product formulas & ingredients
  • Supplier details & pricing
  • Customer data (names, emails, orders)
  • Sales figures & business strategy

Breach of confidentiality may result in disciplinary action or dismissal.

⚖️ Disciplinary & Grievance Procedures

Disciplinary Process

  1. Informal chat with Sat
  2. Formal warning (if behavior continues)
  3. Final warning (if still not improved)
  4. Dismissal (if no improvement)

Grievance Process

If you have a complaint about treatment, contact Sat in writing: sat@byerim.com

✋ Discrimination & Equality

ByErim does not discriminate based on age, gender, race, religion, sexual orientation, disability, pregnancy, or marital status.

ByErim has zero tolerance for bullying, harassment, or discrimination.

💷 Pay & Payment

Your hourly rate is stated in your employment contract.

All rates meet or exceed National Living/Minimum Wage.

Payment is made by direct bank transfer. Check your payslip carefully — if something's wrong, tell Sat immediately.

🔒 Data Protection

ByErim holds personal data on you: name, address, email, bank details, tax info, and performance records.

Your rights:

  • Right to know what data is held on you
  • Right to request a copy (Data Subject Access Request)
  • Right to have data corrected if wrong
  • Right to have data deleted (in some circumstances)

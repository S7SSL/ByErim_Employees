Employee Handbook

Effective April 8, 2026

Welcome

This handbook outlines our policies and procedures. Please read through and familiarize yourself with the information below.

Working Hours

Your working hours are set out in your contract. You're entitled to breaks as required by UK law.

Confidentiality

Information about our products, formulas, suppliers, customers, and business operations must remain confidential. This applies to all company information you access in your role.

Breach of confidentiality may result in disciplinary action.

Conduct & Behavior

We expect professional conduct at all times. This includes respect for colleagues, customers, and our brand.

Serious misconduct may result in disciplinary action, including dismissal.

Discipline & Grievance

Discipline

If there's a conduct issue, we'll address it directly with you. Serious matters follow a formal process with opportunity for your response.

Grievance

If you have a concern, contact your manager. We'll address it fairly and promptly.

Equality & Non-Discrimination

ByErim does not discriminate based on age, gender, race, religion, sexual orientation, disability, pregnancy, or any other protected characteristic.

We have zero tolerance for harassment or bullying.

Health & Safety

We're committed to a safe working environment. Report any hazards or accidents immediately.

Warehouse

All warehouse staff must complete manual handling training. This covers safe lifting and injury prevention for items over 20kg.

Training: High Speed Training - Manual Handling (3 hours, online)

Pay

Your hourly rate is specified in your contract. Payment is by direct transfer.

Data Protection

ByErim holds personal data for employment purposes only. Your data is protected and not shared externally.

Acknowledgment

Please sign below to confirm you have read and understood this handbook.

Working Hours & Flexibility

Standard Hours

  • Employees & Workers: As agreed in your employment contract
  • Part-time roles: Specific hours per day/week/month as documented
  • Flexible roles: Hours may vary week to week based on business needs

Overtime

Overtime is not expected unless agreed in advance. If overtime is needed, discuss with Sat beforehand. Compensation for overtime (if any) will be agreed in writing.

Flexible Working (Employees Only)

  • Employees can request flexible working arrangements
  • Request must be in writing to Sat
  • ByErim will consider all requests fairly
  • Examples: Different hours, working from home, compressed week

Break Entitlements

  • Workers & Employees: 20-minute break for every 6 hours worked
  • Breaks are paid
  • Breaks must be taken during your shift

Annual Leave

Entitlement

  • Employees: 20 days paid annual leave per year (statutory minimum)
  • Workers: 5.6 weeks paid annual leave per year (same as employees)
  • Paid leave is calculated as percentage of hours worked

How to Book Leave

  1. Request leave from Sat as soon as possible
  2. Sat will confirm availability
  3. Once confirmed, it's booked
  4. You'll receive written confirmation

Timing Restrictions

  • Leave is subject to business needs
  • Bank holidays may be counted as leave (to be confirmed with Sat)
  • ByErim will not unreasonably refuse leave requests

Sickness & Absence

Reporting Sickness

If you are unwell and cannot work:
  1. Tell Sat immediately (don't just not show up)
  2. If possible, tell her before your shift starts
  3. If you can't contact Sat, contact BeBe (marge@byerim.com)
  4. Tell her: What's wrong (briefly), when you expect to return, if you need to see a doctor

Statutory Sick Pay (SSP)

  • Employees: Entitled to Statutory Sick Pay (SSP) if off sick for 3+ days
  • Workers: Are NOT entitled to SSP (only employees receive it)
  • SSP is paid at the statutory rate (currently ~£105/week)

Doctor's Certificates

  • If off sick for more than 7 calendar days, you must provide a doctor's letter (Fit Note)
  • Shorter absences: Sat may ask you to confirm you were genuinely ill

Confidentiality & Data Protection

Confidential Information

You must keep the following information confidential:

  • Product formulas & ingredients (ByErim's competitive advantage)
  • Supplier details & pricing (not for sharing with competitors)
  • Customer data (names, emails, addresses, order history)
  • Sales figures & business strategy (not public information)
  • Any information marked "confidential" by management

How to Protect Confidentiality

  • Don't discuss ByErim business with outsiders (friends, family, competitors)
  • Don't share customer data on personal devices or unsecured networks
  • Lock your computer when you step away
  • Don't leave sensitive documents lying around
  • Delete emails with confidential info when no longer needed
Breach of Confidentiality: Serious breach may result in disciplinary action (including dismissal). Legal action may be taken to recover damages.

Social Media & Internet Use

Personal Social Media

You can use social media in your own time, but:

  • Don't post about ByErim without permission
  • Don't share customer/product information publicly
  • Don't claim to speak for ByErim unless authorized
  • Don't post negative comments about ByErim, colleagues, or customers
  • Remember: Posts are public and permanent

Internet Use at Work

  • Internet should be used primarily for work purposes
  • Limited personal use (checking email, quick research) is acceptable
  • Don't access: Adult content, Gambling sites, Illegal content, Anything that distracts from work

Health & Safety

Your Responsibilities

  • Report all hazards and accidents to Sat immediately
  • Use safety equipment provided (if any)
  • Follow safe working practices (especially in warehouse)
  • Don't ignore safety concerns for speed
  • Know your workplace hazards (covered in training/risk assessment)

Accidents & Injuries

  1. Tell Sat immediately — even minor injuries
  2. Get first aid if needed (contact Sat or 999 for emergency)
  3. Document the accident — what happened, when, how
  4. Report to insurance — Sat will file formal accident report if needed

Manual Handling Training

  • All staff handling 30kg+ boxes must complete manual handling training
  • Training is mandatory (legally required)
  • Keep your certificate — proof you were trained

Disciplinary & Grievance Procedures

Disciplinary Process

What Gets Disciplined?

  • Serious misconduct (theft, harassment, violence, gross negligence)
  • Repeated breaches of policy (lateness, absence, poor performance)
  • Breach of confidentiality or data protection
  • Health & safety violations
  • Insubordination or disrespect to colleagues

Process:

  1. Informal Chat — Sat will discuss the issue with you
  2. Formal Warning — if behavior continues
  3. Final Warning — if behavior still not improved
  4. Dismissal — if no improvement

Your Rights in Disciplinary Process

  • Right to know what the allegation is
  • Right to be heard (explain your side)
  • Right to request a meeting with Sat
  • Right to be accompanied by a friend/representative if you wish
  • Right to appeal a decision

Grievance Process

How to Raise a Grievance:

  1. Talk to Sat informally first if possible
  2. Written Grievance to Sat (sat@byerim.com) if needed
  3. Meeting with Sat to discuss and resolve
  4. Response in writing from Sat

Discrimination & Equality

Protected Characteristics

ByErim does not discriminate based on:

  • Age
  • Gender
  • Gender reassignment
  • Race
  • Religion/belief
  • Sexual orientation
  • Disability
  • Pregnancy
  • Marital/civil partnership status

Harassment & Bullying

ByErim has zero tolerance for:
  • Bullying or intimidation
  • Sexual harassment or inappropriate comments
  • Discrimination or prejudice
  • Hostile behavior

If you experience harassment, report to Sat immediately.

Flexible Working (Employees Only)

Who Can Request?

  • Employees only (workers & self-employed cannot request)
  • You must have worked at ByErim for 26+ weeks
  • One request per 12 months

What Can You Request?

  • Different hours (e.g., 3 days/week instead of 5)
  • Different times (e.g., 7am-3pm instead of 9am-5pm)
  • Working from home (if role permits)
  • Compressed week (longer days, fewer days)
  • Job share (if possible)

How to Request

  1. Write to Sat (email)
  2. Explain what you want (be specific)
  3. Explain why you want it
  4. Suggest when it could start
  5. Request a meeting to discuss

Notice Periods & Termination

Resignation (You Leaving)

  • Give notice as stated in your employment contract
  • Typical notice: 1-2 weeks (check your contract)
  • Give notice in writing to Sat
  • Work your notice period (or pay in lieu may apply)
  • Settle final pay, including any accrued leave

Dismissal (ByErim Ending Employment)

  • ByErim will follow disciplinary procedures
  • You have right to appeal
  • Notice period applies unless dismissed for gross misconduct
  • Final paycheck will include settlement

Pay & Payment

Pay Rates

  • Your hourly rate is stated in your employment contract
  • Rates meet or exceed National Living/Minimum Wage
  • Pay includes any annual leave allowance (check contract for details)

How You're Paid

  • Payment frequency is stated in your contract (typically monthly or weekly)
  • Direct bank transfer
  • Payslip will show: Hours worked, Gross pay, Tax & National Insurance, Net pay

Query About Pay

  • Check your payslip carefully
  • If something's wrong, tell Sat immediately
  • Disputes will be resolved fairly

References & Verification

Employment References

  • Upon leaving ByErim, you may request a reference
  • References will be factual and accurate
  • Sat will confirm basic details (dates, role, performance)

Employment Verification

If asked by a future employer or lender, Sat can confirm:

  • Your employment dates
  • Your job title
  • Your salary (if agreed)

Data Protection (Your Information)

What Data ByErim Holds

  • Name, address, phone number, email
  • Bank details (for payment)
  • Tax information (National Insurance number)
  • Health information (if relevant, e.g., disabilities)
  • Performance records

Your Rights

  • Right to know what data ByErim holds on you
  • Right to request a copy (Data Subject Access Request)
  • Right to have data corrected if inaccurate
  • Right to have data deleted (in some circumstances)

How Data Is Protected

  • Kept secure (not shared with outsiders)
  • Only used for employment purposes
  • Deleted when no longer needed (or after 6 years per employment law)
